Once

I've begun looking to poetry (again) in a half-hopeful 
effort to find something that I lost a long time ago.

I make no claim of being a real poet.

I deplore speaking cryptically, and I don't have 
the kind of passion that would enable me to write 
in such a way that I could compare it to birth 
or death or sex or anything that culminates 
in some grand way.

But when everyone else in the house is asleep, and my fingers lie
questioningly upon the home row,

I can almost remember feeling young.
